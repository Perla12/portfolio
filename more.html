    The good friend

      you realize that sometimes 
      even when you feel matured
      you need a helping hand

      Your efforts gets tied to a limit
      and someone special person
      gets the power tountie those limits
      with an authority from abve the skies
      
      A true friend is your other you
      Ready to function when the real you is weak
      when tied to the world's chugging
      mon bonne amie

      Is there to fight the inevitable
      even when you are dead to the world

      how special you are

      Mon bonne amie
